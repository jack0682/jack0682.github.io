Part II collects the theorems that tie ontological structure to
cohomological invariants of the learned state. The aim is not
completeness but orientation: to make clear *why* certain invariants
appear, and in what sense they control the dynamics of the perceiver.

A concrete starting point is the long exact sequence

$$
\cdots \to H^{n-1}(U \cap V) \to H^n(U \cup V) \to H^n(U) \oplus H^n(V) \to H^n(U \cap V) \to \cdots
$$

which recurs in the construction of ontology patches. The chapters that
follow develop it in the context of the ONN latent space.
