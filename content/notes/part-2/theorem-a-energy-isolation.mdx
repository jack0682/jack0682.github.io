> **Prerequisites**: Chapters 2–8 (all of Part I).

> **Theorem A.** For any fruit $F\in\mathfrak{F}_t$:
> $$\frac{\sum_{i,j\in F}W_t(i,j)}{\mathrm{vol}_t(F)}\;\ge\;1-\theta.$$

*Proof.* (Complete; reproduced from Chapter 4.)

**Step 1.** $\mathrm{vol}_t(F)=\sum_{i,j\in F}W_t(i,j)+\mathrm{cut}_t(F,\bar F)$.

**Step 2.** By (F2), $\phi_t(F)\le\theta$. By (F1), $\mathrm{vol}_t(F)=\min\{\mathrm{vol}_t(F),\mathrm{vol}_t(\bar F)\}$, so $\mathrm{cut}_t(F,\bar F)\le\theta\cdot\mathrm{vol}_t(F)$.

**Step 3.** $\sum_{i,j\in F}W_t(i,j)\ge(1-\theta)\,\mathrm{vol}_t(F)$. Divide by $\mathrm{vol}_t(F)>0$. $\square$

**Sharpness.** Equality when $\phi_t(F)=\theta$ and $\mathrm{vol}_t(F)=\frac{1}{2}\mathrm{vol}_t(V)$.
