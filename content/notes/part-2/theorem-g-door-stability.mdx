> **Prerequisites**: Theorem B (door finiteness), perturbation analysis (Chapter 6).

> **Theorem G.** (Complete proof in Chapter 6, Theorem G.)
>
> Under perturbation $\|\delta W\|_\infty$, setting $\epsilon=|V|\cdot\|\delta W\|_\infty$:
>
> (i) $|b'-b|\le|V|\cdot\|\delta W\|_\infty$.
> (ii)--(iv) Doors with margin $>\epsilon$ are stable; only the $\epsilon$-boundary layer may change.
