## Statement

**Theorem (Topology Preservation under Projection-Consensus).**
Let $G = (V, E, w)$ be a weighted ontology graph with Forman--Ricci curvature $\operatorname\{Ric\}_F$ computed on each edge. Let $T = T_\text\{proj\} \circ T_\text\{consensus\}$ be the ONN projection-consensus operator acting on the 64-dimensional fiber space $\mathcal\{F\} = B_\{16\} \oplus F_\{32\} \oplus I_\{16\}$.

Then the LOGOS solver driven by $T$ satisfies:

$$
\operatorname\{CSR\}(T^n(x_0),\; G) \;\xrightarrow\{n \to \infty\}\; 1.0
$$

for every admissible initial state $x_0$, where $\operatorname\{CSR\}$ denotes the Constraint Satisfaction Rate (fraction of ontological constraints satisfied). In particular, the Betti numbers of the persistent-homology filtration induced by the latent embedding are preserved:

$$
\beta_k\bigl(\mathcal\{R\}(T^n(x_0))\bigr) \;=\; \beta_k(G), \quad k = 0, 1, \ldots
$$

once $\operatorname\{CSR\} = 1.0$ is reached.
