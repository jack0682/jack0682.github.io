## What Is Settled

1. **Projection-consensus descent.** The operator $T = T_\text\{proj\} \circ T_\text\{consensus\}$ contracts in the semantic energy metric. Proved operator-theoretically (see [the framework paper](/papers/onn-ortsf-2026/)).

2. **Constraint satisfaction at CSR = 1.0.** The LOGOS solver (Forman--Ricci curvature + Deep Delta Learning + CMA-ES) achieves topology-loss reduction from 11.68 to 1.15 and a 95% success rate, seed-independent across twenty random initialisations (see [constraint-satisfaction paper](/papers/onn-constraint-satisfaction/)).

3. **Constructor determinism.** After the constructor-alias bug fix, 59/59 benchmark runs reproduce deterministically. CI gates P1--P3 (schema lock, determinism audit, semantic safety) enforce this going forward.

4. **Leakage inflation inequality (Lemma L1).** $\lVert M_\text\{off\} \rVert_2 \leq \mu_\text\{diag\} + \eta_\text\{off\} \cdot \lVert M_\text\{off\} \rVert_2$ is proved and forms the basis for the quantile gate.

5. **Cohomological framework (three-axis).** Intrinsic cohomology, relative cohomology (with rank formulas), and persistence barcodes are related by a long exact sequence and computable in polynomial time (see [cohomology paper](/papers/axiomatic-cohomology-relations/)).
