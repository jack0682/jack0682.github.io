Perception is rarely the focus of control-oriented accounts; it is
treated as a pre-step whose output is a vector to be acted on. This
track takes the opposite view: that the *shape* of the perceptual
process is itself what we should analyse, and that the geometry of
its state space dictates which control laws are even admissible.
