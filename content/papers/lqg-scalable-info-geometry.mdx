## Overview

Third in a sequence on LQG with explicit internal time. This paper
tackles the scalability question: can the framework, previously
validated on 2-node toy models, run on networks of ~100 nodes while
preserving physical validity?

## What the paper shows

- **Universal internal-time convergence** — a crossover at N ≈ 25
  beyond which total internal time T_total saturates near ≈ 5–6, a
  genuinely system-size-independent timescale for quantum cosmological
  bounces.
- **Sub-quadratic scaling** — algorithm complexity T_comp ∝ M^{1.74}
  (with M edges), tractable on standard workstations.
- **Singularity resolution robustness** across random, cubic-lattice,
  and scale-free topologies.
- Delivered with ~7,850 lines of open-source implementation.
